About Me

Hello! My name is Rodrigo Zapata. Im a spanish programming graduate based in Newcastle-upon-tyne. I have recently completed my BSc Course in Computer Science and Games design in Northumbria University. I have achieved first class honours and have worked on mutliple projects throughout my stay in university. I have done multiple videogames, from its code to its sound design, participated in a game jam, created a game controller designed for the disabled, designed or contributed to multiple website projects and have even worked with tools like MATLAB with machine learning. I aim to create projects that improve someones day and allow wider accessibility for everyone. Click the buttons below to access my CV and other documents.

My hobbies include competitive online videogames and cooking. I particularly enjoy cooking and im currently looking at joining a cooking course to expand on my skills. I regularly cook healthy (or unhealthy, depends on what I want that day) meals and like to innovate, and iterate on my experiments from my day prior. My work in the kitchen often translates well to my work on the screen. I have done volunteer work for a group of nuns, feeding the local pool and homeless of my area as well as being an english teacher to kids in less fortunate areas of Madrid. I have done 2 years of teacher assistance during my IB studies. Throughout my life i have learned teamwork, compassion, pacience, and creativity.
